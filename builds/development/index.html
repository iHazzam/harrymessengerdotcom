My Portfolio

Below are some examples of work I've developed over the last few months and years. As I'm a student, this portfolio is forever growing and evolving - and your site could be the next one to be featured!

The Printed Cup Company - Order Tracking

I was hired by TPCC to develop additions to their order tracking site. On arrival at the company, I carried out a short ethnography and series of interviews with all of the users of the product, enabled by the factory and the sales office being in such close proximity. I then compiled a list of potential updates, and, according to a priority order decided by management, implemented some changes, including an entire new database-driven process flow of events, new aspects to the system such as faster lead times, custom error messages as well as completely rewriting the automated email dispatch system and the emails themselves. The system was developed in Laravel 4.1 and inspired my passion for back-end web dev to another level.

The Wedding Cup Company

Although not actually involved in writing the code for the site, from conception of concept to deployment of the finished site, I undertook to project manage the site. This involved setting timescales and deadlines, meetings both over the phone and in person, and working heavily alongside the developers and the marketing/graphic designers to ensure the site was built exactly how the client (TPCC, during the internship) wanted. I also took care of all of the hosting, email and deployment of the project. The project was completed on schedule and came in under budget.

Holiday App

My first foray into PHP development, I was hired to create a locally hosted PHP app for a small company to manage their internal holiday requests calendar through. The goals were to provide scalable, adaptable functionality without the extra clutter provided by many off-the-shelf applications. The project was a success, and the company were pleased with the product.
